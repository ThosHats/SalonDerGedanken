About this Salon

Join us for an evening of deep intellectual exploration at the "Salon der Gedanken." This month, we delve into the intersection of modern ethics and artificial intelligence. How do our traditional philosophical frameworks adapt to a world governed by algorithms?

Led by Dr. Elena Fischer, the matinee encourages open dialogue, critical thinking, and a communal search for meaning in the digital age. No prior philosophical training required—just an open mind and a passion for inquiry.
